Andressa

How we met

We met each other on the way to work. I (Andressa) was going to my job interview and, when getting out of the train, I asked Marc and his colleagues if they knew where exactly the company was. The worked in a different company, but they showed me the way.

After almost four months, on my third day of work, we met at the train station and talked all the way to work. Those 15 minutes were enough to know that something good was about to come.

Since then, its been almost two years and a half of a relationship with a lot of love and companionship. We are sure that we want to spend the rest of our lives together and we are very happy to celebrate this moment with all of you!
